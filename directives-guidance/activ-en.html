Hiking

Kluane's most popular activity, from short strolls to multi-day route-finding adventures. Get out for an hour, a day, or a week.

Camp at Kathleen Lake

Pitch your tent and bask in the legendary midnight sun. Just a short drive from town, where sparkling waters are backed by the Kluane Range.

Rafting the Alsek River

Fed by glacial meltwaters from the world’s second highest coastal mountain range, the Alsek River offers extraordinary wilderness travel.
