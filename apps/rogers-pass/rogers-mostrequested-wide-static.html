How to use this map

Rogers Pass Backcountry Access

Red areas are illegal to enter today and extremely dangerous as explosive avalanche control work is planned to keep the highway and railway open.

Yellow areas will not receive explosive avalanche control today. You can access these areas with a FREE Winter Permit and National Park Pass.

Green areas are outside the highway avalanche control program and can be accessed with a National Park Pass.

Winter Permit & Park Pass

Pick up your Winter Permit and National Park Pass at the Rogers Pass Discovery Centre. National Park passes are also available at the Parks Canada office in Revelstoke and online.

To enter any of the yellow areas, you need a FREE Winter Permit.
You need a National Park Pass when visiting Rogers Pass in Glacier National Park.

Parking

All parking lots, except the Illecillewaet Valley Trailhead and the Rogers Pass Discovery Centre require a Parking Permit.

Parking is not allowed in this parking lot today.

A FREE Parking Permit for your vehicle dashboard accompanies your Winter Permit.

Need more information on Winter Permits?

Go to the Rogers Pass Discovery Centre.
