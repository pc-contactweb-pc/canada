Batoche National Historic Site

Journey back to 19th century Métis settler life on the banks of the South Saskatchewan River. Learn about how a traditional way of life changed forever at the final battlefield of the Northwest Resistance of 1885, scene of Louis Riel’s last stand.

Gallop through time to meet the townsfolk of Batoche and learn about life in the 1800s.
