Parks Canada

Parks Canada administers 171 national historic sites, 48 national parks, 5 national marine conservation areas, and 1 national urban park.

Free admission and discounted overnight stays

Parks Canada administers one of the finest and most extensive systems of natural and cultural heritage places in the world. Your gateway to nature, history, and 450 000 km2 of stories.
